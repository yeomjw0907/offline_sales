# Terms Outline

## Purpose

This document defines the structure for the public terms page used by the partner operations platform.

## Recommended Sections

### 1. Service Definition

- describe the service as a partner recruitment and operations platform for the Ready Talk partner program

### 2. Eligibility

- users must provide accurate application information
- service access may be limited by operator approval

### 3. Account and Access

- Kakao login is used for sign-in
- users are responsible for account access control on their Kakao side
- operator may restrict access for abuse or policy violations

### 4. Partner Approval and Status

- application submission does not guarantee approval
- operator may approve, reject, deactivate, or reactivate partners
- deactivation does not erase valid historical performance or settlement records

### 5. Referral and Performance Rules

- referral credit is tied to the approved partner referral code
- performance is counted at the store level
- performance is confirmed at pilot start
- already-attributed store records are not reassigned by default
- operator may correct records in exceptional cases

### 6. Settlement and Payout

- base amount per confirmed case is 20,000 KRW
- payout may be subject to 3.3% withholding
- monthly operational settlement point is the 10th
- payout timing may depend on account info submission and internal review

### 7. Partner Obligations

- do not misrepresent the service
- do not use fraudulent or abusive acquisition methods
- do not submit false store records

### 8. Service Materials

- operator may provide scripts, leaflets, links, and other materials
- materials are for partner activity use within the program scope

### 9. Suspension and Termination

- operator may suspend access for abuse, fraud, or policy violation
- necessary records may be retained after termination for accounting or legal reasons

### 10. Limitation and Changes

- operator may modify service features, processes, or materials
- operator may update operational rules with notice

### 11. Contact

- customer support or operations contact
- notice channel

## Implementation Notes

- The terms page should stay aligned with actual operational policy in `docs/product/operations.md`.
- If payout rules change, update both the public terms page and settlement logic references.
